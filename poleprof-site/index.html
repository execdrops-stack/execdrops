Meet Your Coach

Hi, I'm April —
The Pole Professor

I'm April, an award-winning pole artist, aerial pole performer, and certified pole dance instructor based in Central Florida. With over a decade of performance, competition, and teaching experience, I specialize in dynamic pole movement, aerial tricks, partner flips, aerial dance performance, and artistic choreography.

My coaching is professional, clean, and art-forward by design: strength, grace, storytelling, and athleticism for students who want to build skill with confidence and intention.

My coaching philosophy: build strength, confidence, and creative expression at your pace. First-timer or seasoned poler, I meet you where you are.
