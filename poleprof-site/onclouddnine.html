The Story

Made by a Pole Dancer, for Pole Dancers

On Cloud Nine was born from a simple idea: pole dancers deserve apparel that actually gets it. Not generic activewear with a vague "empowerment" message — real pieces made by someone who has gripped the pole, stuck the move, and felt that euphoric moment when everything clicks.

That feeling? That's Cloud Nine. And that's exactly what we're bottling up.

Created by April Piazza, award-winning pole athlete and founder of Pole Proficiency Aerial Fitness in Central Florida.
