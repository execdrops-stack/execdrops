Meet Your Coach

Hi, I'm April —
The Pole Professor

I'm an award-winning pole athlete and certified coach based in Central Florida. With over a decade of performance, competition, and teaching experience, I specialize in dynamic pole movement — aerial tricks, partner flips, and artistic choreography.

My coaching philosophy: build strength, confidence, and creative expression at your pace. First-timer or seasoned poler, I meet you where you are.
